Template Chameleon
==================

As the same animal, this template adapts to its environment.
Whether smartphone, tablet PC, netbook, laptop or classic PC, the
Chameleon template makes your store always looks good.

The precalculated department's menu provides a high speed page loading,
and also in many product groups.

The main product groups* are displayed horizontally with an icon. Sub-categories
work on when you move the mouse.

*We recommend exactly 10 main product groups for optimal display or by
10 divisible number of product groups.
