Template Planets
================

The new template-series "Planets" expands our range of fancy templates
for GS ShopBuilder once again with another pretty template.

A special highlight is the one row header, which integrates
the shop-logo, the fulltextsearch, the minibasket and the mainmenu
in one line.

The fulltextsearch and the login-prompt smoothly scroll out of the
header row, when the icon or the text were clicked.
